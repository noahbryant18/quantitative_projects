This is my attempt at implementing the Carr-Madan methodology of using FFTs in option pricing. Specifically, I am implementing their suggested method of Variance-Gamma.
The project was a good initial attempt, I was unfamiliar with a lot of the "option maths", so its quite likely things are inefficient. The general result seemed to be ok. I used SPY option data from Yahoo Finance.
The link to the paper I found on the NYU website is: https://engineering.nyu.edu/sites/default/files/2018-08/CarrMadan2_0.pdf
